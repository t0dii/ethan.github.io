    • About Us Page

    Established in 2009, Flowers Across Melbourne aims to provide a quick, convenient service delivering flowers throughout Melbourne, including flowers to Melbourne CBD and outer Melbourne Suburbs.

    We have quickly established ourselves as Melbourne's premier online florist by catering specifically towards delivering fresh, premium quality flower arrangements and bouquets throughout Melbourne, therefore allowing us to offer a friendly local service whilst ensuring high standards of quality and great prices. Unlike many online florists who use relay services, all of our flowers are delivered direct from us. When you pick up the phone or email us, you will be dealing directly with one of our friendly and experienced florists.

    Flowers Across Melbourne is an online store which means we are not open to the public, allowing us to keep our prices affordable, however, we do offer a pickup option (please call to arrange pickup).

    If you need flowers sent outside of Melbourne, Sydney and Brisbane metro areas, visit our online store Flowers Across Austalia to send flowers to anyone in VIC, NSW, QLD and SA directly via post!

    If you would like to speak to us, please call us on 03 9999 7997 (+61 3 9999 7997 from outside Australia), or send us an email
